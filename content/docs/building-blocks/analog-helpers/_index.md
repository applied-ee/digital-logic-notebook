# Analog Helpers

**Digital systems still touch the analog world.**

No real system is purely digital at its edges. Something has to decide when a continuous voltage crosses a threshold, route an analog signal under digital control, hold a sample steady long enough to convert it, or condition a signal before it ever reaches a logic input. These parts sit on the boundary and are indispensable even in otherwise all-digital designs.
